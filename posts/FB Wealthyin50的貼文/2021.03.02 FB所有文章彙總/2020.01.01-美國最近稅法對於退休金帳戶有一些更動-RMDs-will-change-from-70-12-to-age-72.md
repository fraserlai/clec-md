[ 美國最近稅法對於退休金帳戶有一些更動， (RMDs) will change from 70 1/2 to age 72 ] CLEC 投資理財教育頻道 博文日期: January 01, 2020

美國最近稅法對於退休金帳戶有一些更動，兩個重點供大家參考，詳細細節可以參考全文：

Retirees — Required minimum distributions (RMDs) will change from 70 1/2 to age 72

Beneficiaries — There will be a 10-year time limit for a non-spouse beneficiary to defer the distributions and income taxes on an inherited IRA.
