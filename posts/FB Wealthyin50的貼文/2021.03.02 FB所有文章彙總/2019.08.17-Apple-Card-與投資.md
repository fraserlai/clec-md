【Apple Card 與投資! 】CLEC 俱樂部 撰寫日期: August 17, 2019

昨天收到Apple 申請 Apple card 邀請【You're invited to get Apple Card starting today.】的 email .

現在我已經有Apple Card 了！( 我知道 目前只有幾個國家可以申請Apple Card - 美國、加拿大、澳洲 。)

為何我對Apple Card 如此興奮呢！因為身為投資者投資者，你可以親身感受到你的公司在做的業務和其服務品質，你就會感到無比興奮。

我是透過Apple 網站上的apple card notify me 點選【Notify Me 】之後你就可以填上你Apple ID 的email

[**https://www.apple.com/apple-card/**](https://www.apple.com/apple-card/)

你也可以到APPLE 官網搜尋apple card notify me 找到這連結網站。

確認你的Apple ID email(你可以到iPHone setting 最上方你的名字點進去，在你名字下面就有你的Apple ID email)。

在【Notify Me 】頁面填好你的Apple ID email 你就可以submit 。48小時後你在Apple ID email 就會收到【You're invited to get Apple Card starting today.】的email。

當然你最好先將iPhone 升級到最新iOS 版本。

啟動你的ApplePay 以下是你應該先準備好的動作。

How to get your invitation:

1 Make sure your iPhone is running iOS 12.4. To update,go to Settings > General > Software Update.

2 Take note of the Apple ID used to sign in to iCloud on your iPhone. To see your Apple ID, go to Settings, then tap your name.

3 Set a passcode for your iPhone, if you don’t have one already. To set a passcode,go to Settings,tap Face ID & Passcode or Touch ID & Passcode, then tap Turn Passcode On.

4 Go to [**apple.com/apple-card**](http://apple.com/apple-card) and tap Notify Me to enter your Apple ID.

Invitations may take up to 48 hours to arrive.
